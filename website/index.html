2026 理大人工智能 x 數位人文獎

2026 PolyU AI x Digital Humanities Awards

以「AI & Classics」為主題，邀請人文學院本科生運用人工智能重新閱讀、分析、展示與教學古典文本、歷史文化和經典思想。

Under the theme "AI & Classics," undergraduate students are invited to reimagine classical texts, history, culture, translation and language analysis through artificial intelligence.

01 · 比賽定位與主題

01 · Positioning & Theme

以 AI 重新詮釋經典材料，連結研究、教學與公共傳播

Reinterpret classics through AI, linking research, teaching and public engagement

「理大人工智能 × 數位人文獎 2026」是香港理工大學人文學院面向本科生的校內比賽，鼓勵學生運用人工智能、數位人文及相關科技，研習古典文獻、經典思想、歷史文化、翻譯與語言分析。

The PolyU AI × Digital Humanities Awards 2026 is an internal Faculty of Humanities competition for undergraduate students, encouraging AI and digital humanities approaches to classical texts, canonical thought, history, culture, translation and linguistic analysis.

本屆主題為「AI & Classics」。Classics 可包括古典文本、經典思想、古代歷史文化、古籍圖像、經典翻譯、古典語言、接受史、經典詮釋與相關知識傳播。

The theme is "AI & Classics." Classics is understood broadly: classical texts, canonical thought, premodern history and culture, rare-book images, translation of classics, classical languages, reception history, interpretation and related knowledge communication.

02 · 參賽資格及組隊

02 · Eligibility & Team Formation

面向人文學院全日制本科生，可個人或 2 至 4 人組隊參賽

Open to full-time Faculty of Humanities undergraduates, individually or in teams of 2 to 4

合資格課程 Eligible programmes

人文學科課程組合、中國歷史及文化、英文及應用語言學、語言學及翻譯學、語言科學與語言數據分析、言語治療等人文學院合資格本科課程。

Bachelor's Degree Scheme in Humanities; Chinese History and Culture; English and Applied Linguistics; Linguistics and Translation; Language Science and Analytics; Speech Therapy and other eligible FH undergraduate programmes.

隊伍人數 Team size

可個人參賽，亦可組成 2 至 4 人隊伍；每隊最多 4 人。

Entries may be submitted individually or in teams of 2 to 4; each team may have no more than 4 members.

跨學科組隊 Interdisciplinary teams

鼓勵上述合資格課程學生跨學科組隊，但所有正式隊員均須來自人文學院合資格課程。

Interdisciplinary teams among eligible FH programmes are encouraged, but all official team members must be enrolled in one of the eligible programmes.

提交限制與核實 Submission limit and verification

每名學生只可作為一隊正式成員參賽，每隊只可提交一份作品；主辦單位保留核實學生身份及參賽資格之權利。

Each student may join only one team as an official member, and each team may submit only one entry. The Organising Committee may verify student status and eligibility.

03 · 作品形式

03 · Accepted Forms

作品不限單一形式，可結合多種成果呈現

Entries may combine multiple forms within one project

01

研究型作品

Research-oriented work

以數位人文技術與量化方法分析古典文獻數據，例如資料集、文本語料庫、圖表、地圖、人物關係網絡、GIS、知識圖譜、翻譯比較或古典文本分析。

Digital humanities and quantitative analysis of data derived from classical texts, such as datasets, corpora, charts, maps, networks, GIS, knowledge graphs, comparative translation or classical text analysis.

02

工具型作品

Tool-oriented work

開發處理古典文獻的工具，如 OCR、NLP 工具、檢索系統、AI Agent 或圖像分類模型。

Tools for processing classical texts, such as OCR, NLP tools, information retrieval systems, AI agents or image-classification models.

03

教學型作品

Education-oriented work

結合數位人文技術與文史知識，設計互動教材、評估工具、教育影片、操作指南或課程單元。

Interactive materials, assessment tools, educational videos, tutorials, operation guides or course modules integrating DH technologies with literary and historical knowledge.

04

展示型作品

Exhibition-oriented work

把古典文獻或歷史材料轉化為沉浸式、互動式媒介，如 AR/VR、虛擬展覽、互動歷史遊戲、3D 復原、數位檔案館、AI chatbot 或多模態敘事。

Immersive and interactive media based on classical texts or historical materials, including AR/VR, virtual exhibitions, interactive historical games, 3D reconstruction, digital archives, AI chatbots or multimodal narratives.

* 所有作品須清楚回應「AI & Classics」主題，並說明其教育價值、研究價值或公共傳播價值。
參賽作品及提交材料可用中文或英文撰寫及展示。

* All entries must clearly address the theme "AI & Classics" and explain their educational, research or public communication value.
Entries and submission materials may be written and presented in either Chinese or English.

04 · 提交要求

04 · Submission Requirements

報名、初賽與入圍後提交，逐步從構想到可示範作品

From registration to first-round and final submission, moving from concept to demonstrable work

1

報名階段

Registration stage

2026年9月30日前填妥網上報名表，提交隊伍資料、學生編號、就讀課程、聯絡人、暫定題目、作品類型及與主題關聯的一句說明。

By 30 September 2026, complete the online form with team details, student IDs, programmes, contact person, provisional title, submission type and a one-line connection to the theme.

2

初賽提交

First-round submission

2026年12月31日前提交作品摘要、PDF/PPT 簡報、AI 使用聲明、資料來源聲明，以及可展示的初步版本或原型；可未完成，但不可只有文字描述。

By 31 December 2026, submit the abstract, PDF/PPT deck, AI Use Statement, Source Declaration and a demonstrable preliminary output or prototype; unfinished work is acceptable, but text-only description is not.

3

入圍後最終提交

Final submission after shortlisting

入圍隊伍須提交最長 5 分鐘 MP4 作品短片、修訂版最終簡報、示範版本或連結；研究型或教學型作品另須提交 1,000 至 1,500 字作品報告。

Shortlisted teams submit a project video of up to 5 minutes, revised final slides and a demonstrable version or link. Research- or education-oriented entries also provide a 1,000-1,500 word report.

05 · 評審準則與程序

05 · Judging Criteria & Procedure

六個維度衡量：關聯、技術、創新、學術、責任與表達

Six dimensions: relevance, technology, innovation, scholarship, responsibility and presentation

20%問題界定與 Classics／Humanities 關聯Problem definition and relevance to Classics / Humanities
20%可行性、功能性與技術實踐Feasibility, functionality and technical realisation
20%創新與創意Innovation and creativity
20%學術質素與研究嚴謹度Scholarly quality and research rigour
10%社會影響、可持續性與負責任 AISocial impact, sustainability and responsible AI
10%展示與表達能力Presentation skills

評審程序包括初賽材料評閱、公布入圍名單並按意見修訂，以及決賽簡報與示範。每份入圍作品原則上由不少於 3 名評審審閱，並可邀請校外專家參與。

Assessment includes preliminary review, shortlist announcement and revision, followed by final presentation and demonstration. Each shortlisted entry will normally be reviewed by no fewer than three judges, with external experts invited where appropriate.

10 · 規則與常見問題

10 · Rules & FAQ

AI 使用、學術誠信、版權與公開展示要求

AI use, academic integrity, copyright and public display requirements

AI 使用與學術誠信有何要求？What are the AI-use and academic-integrity requirements?

所有作品必須為參賽者原創。使用生成式 AI 時，須申報工具、提示詞、生成內容範圍及人工修訂情況；未申報 AI 內容、虛假來源、捏造文獻或偽造引文可導致取消資格。

All entries must be original. If generative AI is used, teams must disclose tools, prompting, generated-content scope and human revision. Undisclosed AI content, fabricated sources or falsified citations may lead to disqualification.

決賽是否會核查誠信？Will there be an integrity check at the final?

會。隊伍須能即場解釋、重現並重新運行作品的任何部分，並按要求定位引文原始出處；入圍隊伍須保存提示詞紀錄、版本對照、來源核查截圖等基本工作紀錄。

Yes. Teams must be able to explain, reproduce and re-run any part of their work and locate original citation sources on request. Shortlisted teams should keep prompt logs, version histories and source-verification records.

資料、圖像、地圖或翻譯如何處理？How should sources, images, maps or translations be handled?

所有引用來源均須可追溯至真實原件。使用第三方文本、圖像、地圖、資料集或模型時，須確保合法授權或合理引用，並注意準確性、脈絡、偏差與 AI 限制。

Every cited source must be traceable to a genuine original. Third-party texts, images, maps, datasets or models must be properly licensed or fairly cited, with attention to accuracy, context, bias and AI limitations.

作品知識產權歸誰所有？Who owns the intellectual property?

作品著作權及知識產權原則上屬參賽者所有。參賽者授予主辦單位非專屬、非商業之教育及宣傳用途使用權；如作品含完整程式碼、資料集或具商業化潛力工具，可提交 demo 或限定存取版本。

Copyright and intellectual property remain, in principle, with participants. Teams grant the organiser a non-exclusive licence for non-commercial educational and promotional use. For full code, datasets or commercialisable tools, a demo or limited-access version may be submitted.

我需要有高階編程經驗嗎？Do I need advanced programming experience?

不需要。比賽將提供工作坊、示範影片、範例作品與常見問題頁面；參賽者可從現成 AI 工具、文本分析與教學設計起步。

No. Workshops, demo videos, exemplar projects and an FAQ page will be provided. Students may begin with existing AI tools, text analysis and instructional design.
